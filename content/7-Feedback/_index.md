> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is extremely open and comfortable. Everyone is very supportive and willing to answer questions, even after working hours. The workspace is quiet and well-equipped, helping me focus well. I only hope that the team can organize one or two small networking or bonding activities in the future so that new interns can get to know each other more easily.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor provides very detailed guidance, explains clearly when I do not understand, and always encourages me to ask questions. The team members also support and create favorable conditions for me to work smoothly. I especially appreciate that the mentor lets me try and solve problems myself instead of simply giving me the answer.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The workload is closely aligned with the knowledge I studied in university. In addition, I was exposed to many new technologies and areas of knowledge, which helped me strengthen my foundation while also improving my practical skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship helped me significantly improve my teamwork skills, my ability to use project management tools, and my professional communication style. The mentor also frequently shared practical real-world experiences that were very helpful for planning my career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The atmosphere here is wonderful. Everyone works seriously, but the environment is always friendly and enjoyable, without pressure or distance. When there are projects, the whole team comes together to support one another, and the team members always treat interns as official members.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company supports interns with a stipend and offers flexible working hours when needed. In addition, being able to join internal training sessions is a big positive.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
The enthusiasm and friendliness of the mentor and the whole team. The environment is open, making it easy for me to ask questions and learn a lot.

- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
I think the company is already very complete, and there is nothing much to improve.

- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Absolutely yes. Because the FCAJ program provides a very good opportunity for students to experience the real working environment and to learn by doing, rather than just handling routine tasks.
