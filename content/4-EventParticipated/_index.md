&emsp;**Participation format:** Participated online through a YouTube livestream

&emsp;**Role in the event:** Participant

&emsp;**Main content:** Learned about Cloud Thinking, Agentic Platforms, Multi-Agent architecture, Voice AI, DevOps AI Agents, Amazon Q, and security requirements when integrating AI with enterprise data and internal systems.

&emsp;**Outcomes:** Gained practical knowledge of AI and Cloud and developed a better understanding of the role of AI in infrastructure operations, incident response, process automation, recruitment, and enterprise data security.
